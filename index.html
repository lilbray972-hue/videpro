FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is VidSyncPro?

VidSyncPro is a desktop application for TikTok content creators. It lets you process videos, add transcriptions, and publish directly to your TikTok account from your computer.

Is my TikTok account safe?

Yes. VidSyncPro uses TikTok's official OAuth login system. We never see or store your TikTok password. You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.

What data does VidSyncPro access?

Only the data you authorize: your public profile info, your own video list for analytics, and the ability to upload/publish videos on your behalf. No third-party data is accessed or shared.

Which platforms does it run on?

VidSyncPro is a Windows desktop application. Your video files are processed locally on your machine for maximum speed and privacy.
