Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

VidSyncPro ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the VidSyncPro application ("the Service"). This policy applies to all users of our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly:

  • Account connection details when you link your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0
  • Video content and media files you import into the Application for processing
  • Publishing preferences and scheduling settings
  • Support inquiries and correspondence

2.2 Information collected automatically:

  • Application usage data (features used, session duration, error logs)
  • Device information (operating system, hardware specifications relevant to video processing)
  • Performance metrics to improve Application functionality

2.3 Information from TikTok:

  • Public profile information (display name, avatar, bio) via user.info.basic and user.info.profile scopes
  • Account statistics (follower count, following count, likes count, video count) via user.info.stats scope
  • Your own video list with engagement metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) via video.list scope
  • OAuth access tokens required for API authentication

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your TikTok account and display your profile information within the app
  • Upload and publish video content to TikTok on your behalf via video.publish and video.upload
  • Retrieve and display your video analytics and engagement metrics
  • Generate transcriptions and subtitles for your video content
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve Application performance
  • Communicate with you about Service updates and support

4. Data Storage and Security

VidSyncPro is a desktop application. Your video content and media files are processed and stored locally on your device. OAuth tokens for your TikTok account are stored securely on your local machine using encrypted storage. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any data transmitted, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL). We do not store your video content on external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you explicitly authorize content publication to TikTok
  • Service providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the Application (e.g., Whisper AI for transcription), under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process

6. TikTok API Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the scopes you explicitly authorize:

  • Login Kit (user.info.basic): Used solely to authenticate you and display your profile in the app
  • Profile data (user.info.profile, user.info.stats): Used to display your bio, verification status, follower count, and video count in the app dashboard
  • Video list (video.list): Used to retrieve your own videos and display analytics (views, likes, comments, shares)
  • Content Posting (video.publish, video.upload): Used exclusively to upload and publish your videos to your own TikTok account at your request

We do not access any other user's data. We do not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than the features described above. TikTok data is not shared with any third party. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings or within the Application.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lilbray972@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Video content processed through the Application remains on your local device under your control. When you disconnect your TikTok account, we delete the associated OAuth tokens and cached profile data within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, to protect your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Cookies and Tracking

As a desktop application, VidSyncPro does not use browser cookies. Our website (this site) uses only essential cookies required for site functionality. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies on this website.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
